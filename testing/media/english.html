ABOUT US

ABCDE Media Productions GmbH produces all kinds of print and digital media in Mannheim with exclusively trained media designers. Our dedicated and motivated teams are thus involved in the production of many daily and weekly newspapers as well as various magazines. Our focus is on the professional production of advertisements tailored to individual clients. But we also realise the conception, design and production of publishing supplements and books. At ABCDE Media Productions GmbH, absolute reliability and adherence to deadlines throughout the entire production chain are not just empty words, but a top priority business principle.

OUR PRODUCTS

Advertisement set:

With almost 20 years of expertise, the creation and processing of advertisements is part of our daily craft. With production close to the customer, our teams with experience and technical competence can realise your wishes professionally and promptly. We currently produce advertisements for many daily and weekly newspapers from all over Germany, and do so reliably and always in a service-oriented manner. Our motto here is: If you and your customers are successful with the advertisements we produce, then we are successful too.


Layout:

WEB

Online advertising media should always be an important part of a company's marketing mix. The advantage of online advertising media is that success becomes measurable. Special analysis tools can be used, for example, to track exactly how many visitors clicked on your banner and were thus led to the desired target page on the web. The design of such online advertising media is also part of our company's extensive range of services.

SERVICE

Prepress:

With our many years of experience, we offer you a complete service in the area of pre-press. This includes data transfer, acquisition, preparation, processing and maintenance. We check and prepare delivered and created data in all common programmes for further production and supervise the data transfer.


Consulting and production:

Being a partner means being able to provide you, the customer, with professional advice and support throughout the entire production process. From conception to printing service - we are there for you! By networking all areas of the company and against the background of state-of-the-art technology and economical, flexible working methods, we guarantee you flawless results, both in terms of deadlines and technical expertise.
